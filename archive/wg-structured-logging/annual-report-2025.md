# 2025 Annual Report: WG Structured Logging

## Current initiatives and Project Health


1. What work did the WG do this year that should be highlighted?

Some progress was made towards supporting contextual logging in client-go and
related apimachinery packages. SIG Node continued with conversion of the
kubelet to contextual logging.

2. Are there any areas and/or subprojects that your group needs help with (e.g. fewer than 2 active OWNERS)?

The working group has not met on Zoom due to a lack of topics and regular attendees.
Discussions on Slack have also been limited. Therefore the decision has been made
to wind down the working group. Work on structured, contextual logging will continue
as part of SIG Instrumentation.
